Frequently Asked Questions

Most modern smartphones have NFC capabilities built-in:

  • iPhone: iPhone 6 and newer support NFC (though iPhone's NFC is more limited than Android)
  • Android: Most Android phones from the last 5 years have full NFC support

For payments (PayRing), make sure your phone supports mobile payment systems like Google Pay or Apple Pay.

All our kits are designed to be assembled by anyone, even without technical experience. Each kit includes detailed step-by-step instructions with images. The InfoRing and AccessRing are simpler to assemble (15-20 minutes), while the PayRing requires a bit more patience and precision (30-45 minutes).

Yes, when properly assembled according to our instructions, all PayRing kits are water-resistant (IP67 rated), which means they can withstand immersion in water up to 1 meter for up to 30 minutes. This makes them suitable for everyday activities like handwashing, showering, and swimming in shallow water.

The PayRing utilizes the same secure NFC payment chip that's in your credit card. It includes all the security features of modern contactless payment cards, including:

  • Encrypted communication
  • Tokenization (your actual card number is never transmitted)
  • Transaction limits for contactless payments without PIN

Remember that using PayRing is subject to the same terms and conditions as using your physical credit card.

It depends on the kit type:

  • InfoRing: Yes, you can change the linked URL at any time through our platform.
  • AccessRing: Yes, you can reprogram it using the included ESP32 module to copy data from a different access card.
  • PayRing: No, once assembled, the payment functionality is permanently tied to the specific credit card chip used.
