About PayRing

We're revolutionizing wearable technology with our DIY NFC ring kits.

Our Story

PayRing was founded in 2022 by a group of technology enthusiasts who believed that wearable tech should be accessible to everyone. We were frustrated by the high costs and limited customization of existing NFC wearables on the market.

Instead of just complaining, we decided to take matters into our own hands. After months of research, development, and testing, we created our first DIY kit - the InfoRing. The response was overwhelming, and we quickly expanded to include AccessRing and PayRing kits.

Today, PayRing is a leading provider of DIY NFC wearables, helping thousands of users around the world to create personalized technology solutions that fit their unique needs and style.

Our Mission

At PayRing, our mission is to democratize wearable technology by providing high-quality, affordable DIY kits that empower users to create custom NFC rings tailored to their specific needs.

Accessibility

We believe everyone should have access to wearable technology, regardless of technical expertise.

Education

We're committed to teaching people about NFC technology and its practical applications.

Innovation

We continuously improve our kits based on customer feedback and technological advancements.
